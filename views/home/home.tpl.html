Ohio Valley Home Health Services Medicare & Medicaid Certified

We are committed to providing quality health care essential to the improvement of physical, mental, and social conditions of patients and their families within our service area.

Why OVHHS?

Commitment:

OVHHS is committed to providing high quality home care services to patients and their families that meets the needs of the patient while ensuring cost-effective delivery of services.

Principles:

OVHHS is committed to preserving dignity, encouraging independence, showing respect to all, enabling freedom of choice and embracing diversity.

Values:

Provide excellent service
Show Respect to ALL
Embrace Diversity
Ensure Freedom of Choice

What we can do for you

OVHHS is a team of professional Registered Nurses, Licensed Practical Nurses, Physical Therapists and Assistants, Occupational Therapists and Assistants, Speech Therapists, and Medical Social Workers to meet your skilled service needs. All of our staff members are ready to assist you in becoming independent in your home.
